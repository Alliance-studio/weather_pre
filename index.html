Track States — GR86 Baseline
This tool is an Alpha concept we are exploring to help drivers navigate the rain.
Quick reference for tyre choice and strategy by track condition. Use F9 + Weather Radar to monitor in real-time.
Condition (UI) Tyre Strategy / When to change Risk
Dry Slicks Dry track. Maximum grip. No worries. low
Very Lightly Wet Slicks Just dampness. Wet tyres melt here — keep Slicks. low
Slightly Wet Slicks ⚠ Critical transition. If there's a dry line → keep Slicks. If rain is increasing → prepare Wets. medium
Slightly Wet Wet If rain is increasing on the radar — pit for Wets immediately. medium
Wet Wet Visible spray. Wet tyres mandatory. No discussion. high
Very Wet / Damp Wet Beware of puddles. High aquaplaning risk. Reduce speed in low track areas. high
Soaked Wet Rivers on track. Survival mode — avoid kerbs, reduce aggressiveness entirely. critical
F9 & Radar Monitoring
🌡
F9 — Tyres
If your Wets are orange/red, the track is drying. Look for puddles to cool them or pit for Slicks.
👁
Visual Spray
Cars ahead stopped lifting spray? The track is drying. Consider switching to Slicks.
📡
Weather Radar
Yellow/Orange patches on radar = heavy rain. Prepare Wets before it hits your sector.
🏁
Drying Track
Running Slicks on the dry line (even if wet off-line) yields significant lap time gains.
